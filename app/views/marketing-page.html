Solution description

Solution description
Summary

Solutions Document Manager is one of the most popular document management suites in the UK. Our system is designed to be a complete document management solution that focuses on usability and clinical safety.
About the Solution

Solution Document Manager has been used across the UK since 1980. With nearly 300,000 patient using it annually, it is one of the most relied upon Solutions in the market.

www.solutions.co.uk/document-manager
